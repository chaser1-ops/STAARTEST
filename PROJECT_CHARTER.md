# Project Charter — STAARTEST

## Mission
Establish STAARTEST as a clean, versioned, production-ready project with a strong static web foundation, GitHub integration, Netlify deployment readiness, and long-term continuity for a future iOS app companion.

## Scope
- **Website**: STAAR Power Portal for Grades 3–6 (Reading + Math)
- **iOS App**: Future companion app (H1STAARTESTAPP repo)
- **Hosting**: Netlify with GitHub-backed continuous deployment

## Target Audience
- **Primary**: Texas elementary/middle school students (Grades 3–6) preparing for STAAR
- **Secondary**: Parents who want visibility into study progress and practical tools

## Design Principles
- Confidence-first: motivate, don't pressure
- Fun, colorful, and kid-safe
- Practical and parent-useful
- Premium polish, not cheap cartoon chaos
- Short study sessions over marathon cramming

## Brand
- **Product**: STAAR Power Portal
- **Credit**: Brought to you by www.risestudiolabs.com
- **Repos**: STAARTEST (website), H1STAARTESTAPP (iOS)

## Tech Constraints
- Static HTML/CSS/JS only (no build tools)
- localStorage for progress (no backend)
- Responsive layout (desktop + mobile)
- Netlify-safe deployment
