Official Source Notes

This site's design and study structure were informed by official Garland ISD and Texas Education Agency resources.

  • Garland ISD Testing Calendar — used for Grade 3–5 Reading and Math STAAR window dates.
  • Garland ISD STAAR and STAAR EOC — confirms elementary STAAR subjects at grades 3–8.
  • TEA STAAR Released Test Questions — confirms released forms, answer keys, item rationales, and student expectations for each grade.
  • TEA STAAR — confirms online released tests and practice sets.
  • TEA RLA Resources — reading and language arts support materials.
  • TEA Mathematics Resources — math support materials.
  • Texas Assessment — STAAR — family-facing overview of STAAR purpose.
  • Texas Assessment Students and Families — family support hub.
This project uses original kid-friendly study language and original site design. It does not reproduce official test materials.
