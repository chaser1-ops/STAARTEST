What This Site Is

STAAR Power Portal is a free educational support tool created by RISE Studio Labs. It provides practice questions, flashcards, study tips, and printable resources for K-6 students preparing for the Texas STAAR assessment.

Educational Use Only

This site is designed as a supplemental study aid. It is not a substitute for classroom instruction, official test materials, or professional tutoring. We are not affiliated with, endorsed by, or officially connected to the Texas Education Agency (TEA), Garland ISD, or any school district.

No Guarantees

While we strive to provide accurate and helpful content, we cannot guarantee specific test outcomes. Study results depend on many factors including classroom preparation, individual readiness, and test conditions.

Content Accuracy

Practice questions and study materials are original, kid-friendly content inspired by publicly available STAAR skill frameworks. They are not reproductions of official test items. Test dates and school calendar references are based on publicly available information and may change.

Free to Use

This site is free for personal and family educational use. No account is required. No payment is collected. Content may not be redistributed commercially without permission.
