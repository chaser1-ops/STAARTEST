Reading Boost

All Grades
4-Step Answer Strategy

1. Read the question first.
2. Find proof in the passage.
3. Cross out weak answers.
4. Pick the answer with the best proof.
Quick Reference

Detail: What proves it?

Memory Systems

Grade-Specific Memory Tricks

Quick-recall tools for reading and math. Teach these early and repeat often.

GRADES 3–4 • READING

P.A.W.

Passage • Answer choices • Why does the text prove it?

Slows you down just enough to find proof before choosing.

GRADE 5 • READING

S.T.A.R.

Scan the question • Text evidence • Answer • Recheck

For complex passages with deeper analysis.

GRADE 6 • READING

C.L.A.I.M.

Claim • Link to text • Analyze • Infer • Main point

For argumentative and analytical reading.

ALL GRADES • MATH

R.U.N.

Read the problem • Underline the target • Number sentence

Turn the story into math. Then solve it.
