Learning Zone

Grade 2 skills and strategies.

Understanding Stories

  • Main idea: What is the story mostly about?
  • Retelling: Tell the beginning, middle, and end.
  • Characters: What do they say and do? That shows who they are.

Vocabulary

  • When you see a new word, look at the words around it for clues.
  • Pictures can help you figure out what a word means.
  • Try using the new word in your own sentence!

Text Features

  • Headings tell you what a section is about.
  • Bold words are important — pay attention to them.
  • Pictures and captions give you extra information.

Making Predictions

  • Use clues from the story to guess what happens next.
  • Ask: "Based on what I read, what makes sense?"
  • Check your prediction as you keep reading!

Addition & Subtraction

  • Add and subtract within 100.
  • Regroup when ones go over 9 (carry to tens).
  • Use mental math: break numbers into tens and ones.

Skip Counting

  • Count by 2s: 2, 4, 6, 8, 10...
  • Count by 5s: 5, 10, 15, 20, 25...
  • Count by 10s: 10, 20, 30, 40, 50...
  • Skip counting helps with multiplication later!

Measuring

  • Use a ruler to measure length in inches or centimeters.
  • Start at zero, not the edge of the ruler!
  • Estimate first, then measure to check.

Fractions

  • A half means 2 equal parts.
  • A fourth means 4 equal parts.
  • The parts must be equal — same size!
