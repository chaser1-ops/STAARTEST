Grade 6 skills and strategies.

Analytical reading, algebraic thinking, ratios, and data analysis. Level up.

Grade 6 reading focus

  • Analysis: How does the author use language to create effects?
  • Argument: Identify claims, evidence, and reasoning.
  • Tone and mood: What feeling does the author create?
  • Figurative language: Metaphors, hyperbole, idioms, allusion.

Critical reading strategies

  • Ask: What is the author's argument or position?
  • Evaluate: Is the evidence strong or weak?
  • Compare: How do two texts treat the same topic differently?
  • Always cite specific text when explaining your answer.

Memory Trick: C.L.A.I.M.

Claim • Link to text • Analyze • Infer • Main point

Writing response tips

  • State your claim clearly in the first sentence.
  • Use at least two pieces of text evidence.
  • Explain how each piece of evidence supports your claim.
  • Use academic language: "The author demonstrates...", "This suggests..."

Grade 6 math zones

  • Ratios and proportional reasoning
  • Percentages, fractions, and decimals
  • Algebraic expressions and equations
  • Integers and the number line
  • Area, surface area, and volume
  • Data analysis: mean, median, mode

Key strategies

  • Set up ratios as fractions and cross-multiply.
  • Translate words to math: "is" = equals, "of" = multiply.
  • Use inverse operations to solve equations.
  • Plot on number lines to understand negative numbers.

Memory Trick: S.O.L.V.E.

Study the problem • Organize info • Line up a plan • Verify your work • Explain your answer

Check yourself

  • Did I define my variable?
  • Did I simplify fully?
  • Does my answer make sense in context?
  • Did I check with inverse operations?

Night before

  • Organize materials.
  • Review key formulas only.
  • Sleep early.

Morning of

  • Fuel up with a good meal.
  • Positive visualization.
  • Arrive prepared and calm.

During the test

  • Budget your time — don't get stuck.
  • Show all work.
  • Use process of elimination.

Mindset

"I've done the work. I trust my process. Let's go."
