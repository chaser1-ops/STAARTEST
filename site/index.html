The big idea

Keep it visual. Keep it short. Repeat the important parts. Win the next small step.

This portal is designed like a mission control center instead of a boring worksheet dump. It breaks studying into colorful bite-size blocks and gives students a clear path: learn it, practice it, print it, repeat it.
