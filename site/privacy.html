Privacy

Last updated: April 4, 2026

We Don't Collect Your Data

STAAR Power Portal does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. There are no accounts, no logins, no email collection, and no analytics tracking.

Local Storage Only

Your study progress, profile (nickname and avatar), streaks, and badges are saved in your browser's local storage on your device. This data never leaves your device and is never sent to any server.

No Cookies

This site does not use tracking cookies, advertising cookies, or third-party analytics. We use browser localStorage for study progress only.

No Ads

There are no advertisements on this site. No ad networks, no sponsored content, no tracking pixels.

Kid Safe

This site is designed for children and families. We do not collect personal information from children. All content is age-appropriate and educational.

PDF Downloads

PDF power packs are downloaded directly to your device. We do not track which PDFs are downloaded or by whom.

Hosting

This site is hosted on Netlify. Netlify may collect standard web server logs (IP addresses, page requests) as part of normal web hosting. See Netlify's privacy policy for details.
